My favourite hobby


Can A Hobby Make You Happy?

My favourite hobby is reading. Reading is a worldwide, enjoyable hobby and reading different kinds of books is a part of our lives. It is the best medicine! It changes your perspectives and helps you to acknowledge different cultures and languages while making you happy.

Benefits Of Reading

Readers have a strong imagination and a good memory as their vocabulary improve and learn new words. You can decide quickly and easily as your problem solving and decision making skills improve. Furthermore, you can learn the countries and you can chat with other book worms about books. Reading book is the world's international language. As a list, you can learn;

  1. Cultures
  2. Perspectives
  3. Different languages
  4. New vocabulary
