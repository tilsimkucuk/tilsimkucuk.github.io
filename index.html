Can A Hobby Make You Happy?

My favourite hobby is reading. Reading is a worldwide, enjoyable hobby and reading different kinds of books is a part of our lives. It is the best medicine! It changes your perspectives and helps you to acknowledge different cultures and languages while making you happy.

Readers have a strong imagination and a good memory as their vocabulary improve and learn new words. You can decide quickly and easily as your problem solving and decision making skills improve.
